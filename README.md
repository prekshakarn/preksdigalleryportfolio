## Portfolio Website for Instagram Content Creator

A clean, aesthetic, single-page portfolio site for a lifestyle, skincare, and beauty Instagram creator. Designed to showcase brand collaborations, metrics, services, and contact details in a way that attracts brand partnerships.

### How to open

- **Option 1**: Double-click `index.html` to open it in your browser.
- **Option 2**: From the terminal:
  - `cd /Users/preksha/Desktop/Protfolio`
  - Run a simple local server, for example:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8000
```

Then visit `http://localhost:8000` in your browser.

### Customization guide

- **Name & title**
  - Update the `<title>` tag and hero copy in `index.html` with your own name and positioning.
- **Images**
  - Replace the hero and content section image URLs in `styles.css` with your own hosted images, or local images placed in an `assets/` folder.
- **Metrics**
  - In the **Metrics** section of `index.html`, update follower count, engagement, and any other stats to match your current analytics.
- **Brands & case studies**
  - In the **Brand collaborations** section, replace placeholder brand names and bullet points with your real collaborations and results.
- **Services**
  - Adjust service names, bullet points, and offerings to match how you like to work with brands (e.g., pricing ranges, formats, or deliverables).
- **Contact details**
  - Update the email, Instagram handle, and location in the **Contact** section.

### Files

- `index.html` – Structure and content for the portfolio.
- `styles.css` – Visual design, layout, and responsive styles.
- `script.js` – Smooth scrolling for nav links and a simple contact form UX (no backend; it shows a confirmation message only).
