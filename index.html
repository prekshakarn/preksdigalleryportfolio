About

Meet the face behind @preks_digallery

I'm Preksha, the creator behind @preks_digallery — a space where lifestyle, skincare, and beauty come together in soft tones, real routines, and calming visuals. I love translating products into everyday moments that feel personal, not pushy.

My content leans into honest reviews, textures, and simple rituals that viewers can actually imagine in their own lives. From GRWMs and transition reels to "get ready with me for the day" moments, each piece of content is created to be saveable, shareable, and shoppable.

I partner with brands who care about thoughtful storytelling, long-term relationships, and content that blends naturally into the feed while still driving real campaign results.
